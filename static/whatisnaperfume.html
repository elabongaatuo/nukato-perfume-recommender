Notes on Notes

Perfume Theory
Just like onions, your perfume also has layers:
three to be exact. These layers are called notes each
with unique ingredients/scents that work together
to create your favorite perfume.

Top Notes:
First impressions matter.
This is what hits your nose after the first spray and
are evaporate fast

Heart Notes:
These are transitional notes that give your perfume
character. They aid in transitioning from top notes
to base notes and make up 50% to 70% of your
perfume

Base Notes:
This is the scent that carries you through the day.
That last whiff that lingers and is your signature smell
They are last to evaporate.
